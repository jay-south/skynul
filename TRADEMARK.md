# Trademark Policy

This document describes how you may use the "Skynul" name, logos, and other
brand assets ("Skynul Marks").

## Quick rules

- You may fork and modify the code under the Apache-2.0 license.
- You may refer to the original project to describe compatibility or origin
  (nominative use), as long as it is clear you are not affiliated.
- You may not use the Skynul Marks in a way that suggests endorsement,
  affiliation, or an official relationship.
- If you distribute a forked product, you must use your own name and branding.
- Do not use Skynul logos or visual identity in your forked distribution.

## Examples

Allowed:

- "Fork of Skynul" in documentation.
- "Compatible with Skynul" in a technical description.

Not allowed:

- Naming your product "Skynul" or using confusingly similar branding.
- Using Skynul logos in marketing, app icons, or screenshots for promotion.

## Questions

If you need permission to use the Skynul Marks (for example, for an official
integration, partnership, or redistribution under the Skynul name), contact the
maintainers via the repository.
